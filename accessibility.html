Accessibility

Our commitment to a site that works for everyone. WCAG 2.1 AA conformance is the target.

Effective May 23, 2026

Our commitment

Show Masters Production Logistics is committed to making this website usable by as many people as possible. We aim for conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. We also honor the prefers-reduced-motion media query — if your system has that on, animations on this site (intro scan, between-page curtain, scroll fades, hero crossfade) are disabled.

What we've done

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper headings, landmarks, and form labels.
  • Keyboard navigation supported across all interactive elements.
  • Color contrast targeted at WCAG AA minimums for body copy.
  • All decorative images carry alt="". Content images carry descriptive alt text.
  • Forms include explicit labels, required-field marking, and accessible error messaging.
  • Skip-to-content patterns and ARIA landmarks where appropriate.
  • Reduced-motion preference honored.

Known limitations

This site is under active development. Some surfaces are not yet fully audited (interactive map markers, dynamic filter chips on the Shows page). We're working through them. If you encounter a barrier, tell us — see Contact below.

Standards and approach

Internal targets: WCAG 2.1 AA, Section 508, ADA Title III alignment. We test with: keyboard only, screen reader (VoiceOver / NVDA / JAWS), 200% zoom, high-contrast OS modes, and the prefers-reduced-motion preference. Manual audits happen at each major release.
