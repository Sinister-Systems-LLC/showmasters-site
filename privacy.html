Privacy Policy

How Show Masters Production Logistics handles your information. Plain language. No dark patterns.

Effective May 23, 2026  ·  Last updated May 23, 2026

Plain summary: We collect what we need to give you a quote, process applications, run payroll, and reply to messages. We don't sell data. We don't run ad tracking. Cookies are used only for site functionality and aggregate analytics. You can ask for a copy of your data or have it deleted any time.

1. Who we are

Show Masters Production Logistics, Inc. ("Show Masters," "SMPL," "we," "our," "us") is a live event labor and production logistics company incorporated in Florida. Florida Hub at 4906 Patch Road, Orlando, FL 32822. Texas HQ at 6340 Lake Worth Boulevard, Fort Worth, TX 76135. We can be reached at Orders@ShowMasters.com or (877) 765-2267.

2. What we collect

From visitors: aggregated, non-identifying analytics (page views, referrer, approximate region from IP, device type). No individual profiles.

From estimate requests, crew orders, and contact forms: name, email, phone, company, event details you share, and anything you write in the brief field.

From job applicants (careers.html): name, contact info, work eligibility, work history, references, and any documents you upload (resume, certifications). Application data is reviewed by our hiring team and retained per the timeline in section 6.

From clients during active engagements: billing contacts, venue contacts, insurance certificate-holder details, payroll-related data required to staff the show and invoice you.

From employees: employment records, payroll information, W-4 forms, ACA-compliance documentation, certifications. Handled per applicable employment and tax law.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and produce estimates.
  • To accept and process crew orders.
  • To evaluate and respond to job applications.
  • To staff, payroll, and invoice live event engagements.
  • To meet our legal obligations (tax, labor law, ACA, insurance).
  • To improve our service via aggregate analytics. No individual profiles, no ad targeting.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share what's needed with:

  • Service providers who help us run the business (payroll processor, payment processor, email delivery, hosting). Bound by confidentiality terms.
  • Insurance carriers for workers' comp and general liability coverage on active engagements.
  • Government agencies when required by law (tax filings, wage garnishments, subpoenas, ACA reporting).
  • Clients when staffing their event — limited to the crew member's first name, role, and credentials necessary for venue access.

5. Cookies + tracking

We use a small number of cookies for site functionality (session, preferences) and privacy-respecting analytics in aggregate. We do not run ad-network tracking pixels. We do not share cookie data with advertisers. Details and opt-out instructions are in our Cookie Policy.

6. How long we keep it

  • Contact / estimate inquiries: up to 24 months from last contact, then archived or deleted.
  • Job applications (not hired): 12 months, then deleted unless you've asked to stay on file longer.
  • Active engagement records: 7 years from end of engagement, per IRS / tax retention rules.
  • Employee records: per federal and state retention requirements (typically 3–7 years post-separation).
  • Aggregate analytics: retained indefinitely in anonymized form.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to: see what we have on you, correct it, delete it, restrict how we use it, port it to another service, or opt out of certain processing. To exercise any of these, email Orders@ShowMasters.com with the subject line "Privacy Request" and we'll respond within 30 days.

If you're in California, the EU/EEA, the UK, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights (CCPA, GDPR, UK GDPR), the rights above are guaranteed by law. We don't sell personal information under CCPA's definition.

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, vendor due diligence) to protect personal information. No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach affecting your information, we'll notify you per applicable law.

9. Children

This site and our services are not directed to anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us information, email Orders@ShowMasters.com and we'll delete it.

10. International transfers

We operate from the United States. If you contact us from outside the US, your information is transferred to and processed here. By contacting us, you consent to that transfer.

11. Third-party links

This site may link to third-party services (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, vendor sites). Those services have their own privacy policies — we don't control them and aren't responsible for their practices.

12. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle information, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date at the top. For substantive changes that affect active accounts, we'll notify you directly.
