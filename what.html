Every category of crew
your show actually needs.

Stagehands, riggers, technicians, lift operators, crew leads, show management. Corporate, concert, festival, trade show, convention, sport, theater, house of worship, private.

01

Stagehands

Audio, lighting, video, scenic, deck, set-change. Load-in, cable runs, truss build, pipe and drape, scaffolding, load-out.

02

Riggers

Lead riggers, beam and lift riggers, permanent-point clippers, ground riggers. ETCP-certified. Safety-first, no shortcuts.

03

Technicians

Audio, lighting, video, virtual, breakouts. Systems and engineering specialists. They own troubleshooting and show-readiness.

04

Camera Operators

Hand-held, jib, broadcast multicam, presentation IMAG. Crewed alongside the rest of the video team or on their own.

05

Spotlight Operators

Trained for concerts, theater, awards shows, corporate plenaries. Calm hands, clean follow.

06

Lift & Forklift Operators

Boom fork, boom lift, scissor, forklift. All certified, all insured. Comfortable in active stage environments.

07

Crew Leads

One per shift. Manages pace, performance, paperwork. Signs hours onsite with the client. No billing surprises.

08

Stage Managers

Action management, cue-calling, dressing-room coordination, run-of-show ownership. The clock is the boss.

09

Technical Directors

Full or assisted project management for clients who want a single point of contact across all departments.

10

Show Management

End-to-end leadership for productions that don't have an in-house production lead — we run it for you.

11

Warehouse Crew

Dedicated local warehouse support for ongoing clients with a local truck-pack / staging footprint.

12

Labor Logistics Consulting

Pre-event strategy, cost minimization, post-event evaluation. We help producers tighten the labor line over multi-event tours.

From a breakout room
to a stadium — same standard.

Eight production worlds. One bench of crew. Every show gets the same Crew Lead model, the same insurance bake-in, the same 72-hour invoice.

01 · CORPORATE

Corporate Events

General sessions, breakouts, plenaries, hybrid productions. From a single-day plenary to a five-day annual conference with three stages.

02 · CONCERT

Concerts & Tours

Headliner shows, tour stops, support load-in and load-out, festival rotations. Riggers up the truss, stagehands on the deck, techs on the consoles.

03 · FESTIVAL

Festivals

Multi-stage outdoor builds, IATSE-style rotations, rapid stage changeovers, sand-foot ground rigging. Two days of load-in, two days of show, one night of strike.

04 · TRADE SHOW

Trade Shows & Exhibit AV

Booth installs, install & dismantle, exhibit AV through the AllStar Exhibits I&D division. Forklifts, boom lifts, and crew that already knows the venue layout.

05 · THEATER

Theaters & PACs

Performing arts centers, theatrical productions, touring residencies. In-house crewing contracts at multiple venues. Wardrobe + scenic changeover crews.

06 · WORSHIP

Houses of Worship

Easter, Christmas, special services, ongoing AVL support. We treat the work with the respect, regard, and care the room deserves.

07 · SPORTS

Sporting Events

Indoor and outdoor competitive events, halftime productions, opening ceremonies, broadcast support, national championships.

08 · PRIVATE

Private Events

Weddings, galas, brand activations, executive offsites. High-end parties where production matters and discretion is non-negotiable.
