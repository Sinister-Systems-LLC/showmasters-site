Cookie policy.
What we use. What we don't.

A short list of the cookies that make this site work, what each one does, and how to turn them off. No third-party advertising trackers. No social-network beacons. Just the minimum.

Plain summary: We use a small number of cookies to make the site work and to count traffic in aggregate. We do not run ad-network tracking. We do not sell cookie data. You can decline non-essential cookies in the banner that appears on first visit, or clear them anytime from your browser.

1. What is a cookie

A cookie is a small text file your browser stores when you visit a site. Some are needed for the site to function (remembering you're logged in, your form state). Others are used for analytics or marketing. We use only the first two categories and we use them sparingly.

2. What we use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
smpl_cookies_v1 Functional Records your cookie-banner choice so we don't ask again. 12 months
smpl_intro_* Functional Skips the intro animation between page navigations in the same session. Session only
smpl_paths_* Functional Remembers which paths you've already viewed for the path-tracker floater. Session only
Analytics (aggregate) Analytics Counts page views and approximate region. No individual profiles. Anonymized. 24 months

3. What we do not use

  • No ad-network tracking pixels (Google Ads, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, etc.).
  • No cross-site behavioral profiles.
  • No fingerprinting or super-cookies.
  • No sale of cookie data to data brokers.

4. How to control cookies

On this site: Click "Decline" in the cookie banner on first visit. You can clear all cookies for this site anytime in your browser settings.

In your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data.

Declining functional cookies may affect site behavior (you'll see the cookie banner each visit, the intro animation each page nav, etc.). Site core features will still work.

5. Do Not Track

If your browser sends a "Do Not Track" or "Global Privacy Control" signal, we honor it for analytics. We don't run behavioral tracking regardless.

6. Changes

If we add or change the cookies we use, we'll update this page. Substantive changes will surface a new banner so you can re-consent.
