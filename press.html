Boilerplate.

Show Masters Production Logistics is a live-event labor and crewing company based in Orlando, FL with a Texas HQ in Dallas. Since 2002, SMPL has provided stagehands, riggers, technicians, lift operators, and crew leads to 1,700+ clients across 33 states. Every staffer is a W-4 employee; workers' compensation, general liability insurance, and payroll taxes are baked into the hourly rate. The company operates three divisions: Show Masters Production (live-event crew), AllStar Exhibits I&D (trade-show install & dismantle), and SMPL Systems (audio, video, lighting, control-systems integration).
