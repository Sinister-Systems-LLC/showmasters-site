The people who
show up.

Producers don't book a company — they book the crew. These are six anonymized profiles representative of the people we send to your show. Names redacted for privacy; everything else is real.

CREW LEAD

"D." — Orlando

14 years on the SMPL crew

Corporate plenaries · Concert load-ins · Touring residencies

Started as a deck hand in 2012. Now runs Crew Lead for OCCC + Amway Center repeat-client shows. The Dion mentioned in our home-page testimonial. Producers request him by name on year-over-year bookings — that's why we built the preferred-crew memory system.

LEAD RIGGER · ETCP CERTIFIED

"M." — Dallas

11 years on the SMPL crew

Arena up-rigging · Stadium loads · Touring concert dates

ETCP Theatre Rigging + ETCP Arena Rigging. Came up through IATSE locals before going W-4 with us. Has put truss in the air at every major DFW venue. The walk-through guy who catches a marginal point on the rig plan before the production designer notices.

STAGE MANAGER

"S." — Orlando

9 years on the SMPL crew

Theater + PAC residencies · Touring Broadway · Cue-calling

BFA in Stage Management. Called cues on three touring Broadway productions before joining us full-time. Runs Dr. Phillips Center engagements + most of our theatrical residency contracts. Calm under pressure, fast on the recovery when a cue misses.

AUDIO TECHNICIAN

"J." — Dallas

12 years on the SMPL crew

DiGiCo + Avid consoles · Concert FOH · Corporate plenary mix

DiGiCo and Avid-certified. The audio guy who actually listens to the room and re-tunes mid-show when the temperature shifts. Spent his first career engineering at a Dallas concert venue; we caught him during a contract dispute and have kept him since.

FORKLIFT / BOOM-LIFT OPERATOR · OSHA

"E." — Orlando

7 years on the SMPL crew

Convention I&D · Trade-show booth installs · Forklift, boom, scissor

OSHA-certified across forklift, boom lift, scissor lift. Came in via the AllStar Exhibits I&D division and now runs lift ops on most of our convention work at OCCC. Has logged more freight elevator hours than any one human probably should.

CREW LEAD

"R." — Dallas

16 years on the SMPL crew

Stadium productions · Halftime + pre-game · NFL + MLB ops

Longest-tenured Crew Lead on the Texas roster. Has run AT&T Stadium, Globe Life Field, and Minute Maid Park load-ins more times than he can count. The producer who books him gets the show that's going to start on time.
