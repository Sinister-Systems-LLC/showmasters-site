From brief to invoice

Four steps,
built since 2002.

A process where the crew quietly disappears into the background — because everything goes right. Every Show Masters call, from discovery to 72-hour invoice.

The system

Same playbook,
1,700+ times running.

01

Define the mission

You drop the brief. We assess the show on what actually matters: onsite details, logistical constraints, cost levers, the exact positions and skills required. When techs are involved, we run a real Tech Assessment, not a guess. Every job ends with a same-day itemized estimate so the labor line lands inside your budget.

02

Build the team

From a 130+ W-4 bench plus our hub network, we hand-pick the right crew for your show — not the next-available hands. Lead Riggers paired by venue. Crew Lead matched by show-type. Specialty technicians by console familiarity. The same Crew Lead carries forward on repeat bookings by default.

03

Execute consistently

Crew shows up early with paperwork ready, hard-hats on, comms tested. The Crew Lead carries the radio and runs the floor. Mid-call change orders get assessed and named out loud with their cost so you can decide. The show happens. The crew quietly disappears into the background. That's the whole point.

04

Close it out clean

Hour-12 handoff: room status, open items, owner per item, ETA per item. Itemized invoice in your inbox inside 72 hours of load-out. The number matches the estimate plus any approved change orders. Net-30 standard. No "approximate" entries, no TBDs. Then we book the next one.

No admin percentages.
No surprise fees.

All-in rates that include workers' comp, GL insurance, payroll taxes, and ACA-compliant benefits. The number on the estimate is the number on the invoice.

A

Workers' compensation

Every crew member is covered. You're not buying it. It's already in the hourly.

B

General liability

Full coverage on every shift. COI on request, no upcharge.

C

Payroll taxes

FICA, FUTA, SUTA. All employer-side burden carried by us, included in the rate.

D

Crew Lead time

Crew Lead hours are part of the team you're already paying for. Not a separate management fee.

E

Travel & per-diem

Quoted upfront when crews travel; never tacked on after.

F

Invoice cadence

Detailed invoice within 72 hours of load-out. Net-30 standard, custom terms by request.
